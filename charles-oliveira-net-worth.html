Charles Oliveira’s MMA Career and Achievements

Charles Oliveira began his MMA career in March 2008, quickly making an impact and catching the attention of the UFC. He has competed in multiple weight divisions, including welterweight, lightweight, and featherweight. Throughout his career, Oliveira has achieved notable success, boasting a record of 33 wins and 9 losses.

As a skilled and versatile fighter, Oliveira has achieved numerous milestones and records in the UFC. His achievements include winning the UFC Lightweight Championship, a title he captured in May 2021 by defeating Michael Chandler. Oliveira’s victory made him the first-ever Brazilian to hold the lightweight belt.

In addition to his championship win, Oliveira holds the record for the most submission victories in UFC history, with an impressive total of 16. His submission skills have become a trademark of his fighting style, earning him respect and recognition among fans and fellow fighters alike. Alongside his submission prowess, Oliveira also holds the record for the most finishes in UFC history, with a staggering 19 finishes to his name.
